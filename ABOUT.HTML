Our Story

"Founded by a mom and ECE who wanted a better place for her own children to grow."

Lisa’s Busy Bees was born out of a passion for Early Childhood Education. Founded after COVID-19, our founder wanted to create a "home away from home" where children aren't just supervised, but truly nurtured to learn at their own pace.

Celebrating nearly 4 years of service, we pride ourselves on our play-based learning curriculum and our incredible team of certified educators.
